Microsoft · Growth & Incubation

The Copilot ROI Analytics Team

Formed in early 2025 to meet customers wherever they are in their AI journey. We're People Scientists, Business Value Advisors, Customer Success Managers, and Solutions Architects — working hand-in-hand with the largest Microsoft Copilot deployments in the world.

Over the past year we've shipped a portfolio of custom reports that turn Viva Insights, Microsoft Purview, and Microsoft Graph data into defensible answers about adoption, engagement, impact, and ROI. We measure licensed users, unlicensed users, agentic activity, super-user behavior, and developer habits — full-spectrum coverage of the Copilot ecosystem, grounded in your own tenant data.

We listen, we respond fast, and we ship. Field feedback flows straight back into the Microsoft AI product organization — your real-world questions help shape what comes next.

What we stand for

🧭

Defensible data, always

Every metric we publish can be traced back to a source system, a permission, and a query. No black-box scores. No marketing math.

⚡

Move at field speed

Customer pain points get fast turnarounds. New reports start as a one-off answer to a real question, then get hardened and shared.

🔭

Full-spectrum coverage

Licensed users, unlicensed users, agents, super users, developers — wherever your AI signal lives, we have a lens for it.

🎓

Enable, don't gatekeep

We teach Power BI, VS Code, and AI interfaces to internal teams and customers so they can own the work after we leave the room.

🔁

Two-way with product

What we hear from the field becomes structured feedback into the Microsoft AI product team. Your friction becomes the next feature.

🤝

Real partnership

We work alongside your CSMs, BVAs, and architects — not from a podium. The best results come when we sit on the same side of the table.
