🔑 Cowork Policy Helper · New

Govern Copilot Cowork spend by policy, not by spreadsheet.

Assign every user to a spend-tier billing policy — by department, job family, job title, business unit, or cost center — with ordered first-match rules and a default tier. Review only the exceptions the rules mis-fit, then export a point-in-time record (CSV or slide deck) of who is on which policy and why. Load your own Entra + credit exports right in the browser, or click through the built-in demo. 100% client-side — nothing leaves your device. All figures are planning estimates to help with policy management — moving users between tiers and forecasting how many credits fit current usage — and are not reflective of contracted agreements.
