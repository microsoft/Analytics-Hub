Copilot Cowork Credit — FinOps Cost Report

Load your Entra + Copilot credit exports to generate a FinOps Framework & FOCUS-aligned cost view — entirely in your browser.

All processing happens locally in your browser. Your files never leave your device. No uploads, no servers, no telemetry. Every FOCUS figure is computed on this page from the files you choose.

How it works

1

Export your Entra users

From Microsoft Entra admin center, export the user list as CSV. It supplies the allocation dimensions — department, cost center, business unit, job family, country, and manager.

Expected columns (any subset): User Principal Name, Display Name, Department, Job Title, Job Family, Cost Center, Business Unit, Country / Usage Location, Manager.

2

Export your Copilot credit usage

From the Copilot admin / Cost Management report, export monthly credit usage as CSV. It supplies the consumed quantity — credits used, credit limit, license status, last activity, and session count.

Expected columns: User Principal Name, Monthly credits used, Monthly credit limit, Microsoft 365 Copilot license, Last activity date, Session Count.

3

Generate the FinOps view

The two files are joined on user principal name (UPN) and priced into FOCUS cost columns (List / Contracted / Effective / Billed). Tune the list and contracted rates live, and allocate by department, cost center, or business unit.
